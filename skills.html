software knowledge

Software refers to computer programs or applications that are designed to perform specific tasks or functions. There are various types of software, such as operating systems (like Windows or macOS), productivity software (like Microsoft Office or Google Suite), design software (like Adobe Photoshop or Sketch), and many more. Software is created through programming languages like Python, Java, or C++, and it allows us to accomplish a wide range of tasks, from writing documents to editing photos to playing video games.
